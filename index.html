Crisis-Ready Content

"What do I do right now?" — built into your site

Your future customers Google this exact question while in crisis. We design educational sections that own those searches and position your brand as the expert.

1

Shut off the water source if it's safe to do so

Main shutoff valve is usually near the water meter or where the line enters the home. If you can't find it, skip ahead — the next step matters more.

2

Cut electricity to the affected area

If water is anywhere near outlets or the panel, flip the breaker. Don't stand in water and reach for a switch.

3

Call us — every minute matters for mitigation

Standing water becomes mold within 24-48 hours. We're on-site within 60 minutes for most service areas. Your insurance company expects you to call a mitigation company immediately — that's us.

4

Document everything before cleanup

Photos, videos, timestamps. Your insurance claim depends on it. We'll guide you through documentation when we arrive — it's part of our standard intake.
